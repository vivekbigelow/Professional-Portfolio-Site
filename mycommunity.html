Description

During the summer of 2020 I was part of the 5 week S.O.S. challenge, created by Techpoint. Students were asked to create some form of tech that would help businesses respond to covid. I was assigned the role of a developer and worked on the front-end of a web app, Mycommunity, that helped food banks communicate with their volunteers and consumers via SMS messages.

View Devpost

Interesting Features

  • Group SMS messaging via Twilio
  • Backend database for volunteers and consumers
  • Search

Technologies/Techniques

  • Typescript/Angular.js
  • Django
  • Twilio
